This guide will walk you through the basics of navigating the Black Ops 3 Radiant map editor.

Getting Started

  • Open Radiant via the BO3 Launcher (Map Editor).
  • Use Right Mouse Button to rotate the camera.
  • Use WASD keys to move around the 3D view.
  • Press Shift to move faster.

Viewport Controls

You can switch between views using the toolbar at the top. Common views include:

  • Perspective
  • Top-down Orthographic
  • Side Orthographic
  • Front Orthographic

Tips

  • Press G to toggle grid snapping.
  • Press Ctrl+Z to undo, Ctrl+Y to redo.
  • Use Spacebar to quickly focus on the selected object.
