❌ Fix: Missing XModel

This error means the map references a model that isn’t loaded into your assets.

Solution

  1. Check the model name in the console or compile log.
  2. Locate the model in Asset Manager or your mod’s asset list.
  3. Add the model to your zone_source file: 
    xmodel,your_model_name
  4. Recompile your mod in BO3 Launcher.
